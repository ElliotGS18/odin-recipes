Meatballs Recipe

Description

Juicy and soft!

This easy to cook dish is a classic, best served with vegetables and freshly made tortillas

Ingredients

  • 4 tomatoes approximately 300 grams
  • 3 spoons of rice
  • 300 grams of grounded beef
  • 300 grams of ground pork
  • 3/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of oregano
  • 3 garlic clove
  • 1/4 onion
  • 1/4 cup of chopped onion
  • 4 branches of peppermint chopped
  • 2 eggs
  • Frying Oil
  • 1 teaspoon of powdered beef or chicken broth
  • 1 liter of beef brothoptional
  • 1 big carrot cut in slices
  • 1 squash cut in pieces
  • 1 celery stalk cut in slices
  • 300 gram of potato cut in pieces

Steps

  1. Boil enough water to cover the tomatoes
  2. Add the tomatoes to the water
  3. Wait seven minutes and then turn off the fire and let tomatoes cold
  4. In another pot with boiling water add the rice
  5. Wait 7 minutes and then remove the water from the rice with the aid of a colander
  6. Add the salt and the pepper to the grounded beef and grounded pork, this will be the main mixture
  7. Mill the oregano with your fingers and add it to the main mixture
  8. Press 1 garlic clove and add it to the main mixture
  9. Add the chopped onion and the peppermint to the main mixture
  10. Crack the two eggs and add them to the main mixture
  11. Integrate the main mixture with the aid of a fork
  12. After the mixture looks homogeneous add the rice from the previous steps
  13. Use your hand to integrate the rice into the mixture
  14. After the rice is completely integrated ude your hands to form the balls
  15. To make the sausage, put the boiled tomatoes in a blender
  16. Add the onion, the remaining garlic cloves and 3/4 cup of the tomatoes boiling water
  17. Mix it altogether with the blender, this will be the tomato mixture
  18. Put the stove to work and heat some frying oil
  19. When the oil is warm enough add the tomato mixture
  20. Add the powdered beef or chicken broth, mix it and wait for 7 minutes
  21. Add the liter of beef broth if available, if not, use water
  22. When the tomato mixture starts boiling, carefully add the meatball
  23. After you put all the meatballs reduce the flame intensity and cover the cooking pot
  24. After 20 minutes of cooking add the carrot slices
  25. After 5 minutes of cooking add the squash slices and the celery slices
  26. Add the potato pieces, cover the cooking pot and wait for 17 minutes
  27. Taste the mixture, if it tastes all right turn off the flame
  28. The recipe is READY!!!
