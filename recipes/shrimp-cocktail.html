Shrimp Cocktail Recipe

Description

Fresh and juicy shrimps perfectly cooked This is a very easy to do dish and an easter classic, in tune with the summer and vacations!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 kg of raw peeled shrimp
  • 1/4 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 laurel leaves
  • 8 black peppers
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1.5 cups of ketchup sauce
  • 1/3 cup of lemon juice
  • 1/4 spoon of olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pf orange soda
  • 2 or 3 chipotle peppers
  • 1/4 cup of chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup of chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 teaspoon of "Maggi" sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 chopped avocado

Instructions

  1. Boil 1.25 L of water
  2. Add the onion, the garlic cloves, the laurel leaves, the black peppers and the salt
  3. Mix them together and let them boil for approximately 10 minutes
  4. Add the Shrimps to the mixture
  5. When the color of the shrimps changes from white to a clear pink wait 2.5 more minutes
  6. After that the shrimps should be ready, taste one to be sure
  7. Drain the shrimps with the aid of a colander, save a cup of the water
  8. Remove the onion and garlic lefts and put the shrimps in cold water to stop the cooking
  9. Wait and put the cooked shrimps in a bowl
  10. Mix the chipotle peppers and the orange soda in the blender until its milled
  11. In a bowl mix the ketchup, the previous mixture and the shrimp water
  12. In the same bowl add the lemon juice, the "Maggi" sauce and the Worcestershire Sauce
  13. Mix the previous ingredients until it look homogeneous
  14. Add the chopped onion, cilantro and the shrimps
  15. Then ad the olive oil and mix
  16. Finally add the chopped avocado
  17. The recipe is READY!!!
